About Us:

We cater all occasions from business luncheons to extravagant weddings. We are a family-owned and operated business, and have been since 1994. A Taste of Reality is also a full service Delicatessen featuring Boar's Head provisions.

Our broad catering menu, along with a full array of services, offers the opportunity for you to truly create the kind of event that is exactly what you envisioned. Marianne is the owner and chef for our Catering Department and she is a graduate of New York Restaurant School and has done all of her training in New York City. We would love the opportunity to help you plan your perfect party.

Feel free to give us a call or stop by our store.
